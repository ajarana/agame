How to play

Green and blue blocks are assembled randomly in a 4x4 square. Cure the reddening blocks.

Objective

The goal is to prevent the blocks from turning completely red. Completely red blocks infect adjacent blocks of the same original color. Once all blocks of a color are infected, the game ends.

Rules

When you cure an infected block, the next infected block you cure must be of the same color as the previously cured block, or you will infect it. Once the second block in a pair is cured, you may cure any infected block again. Fully infected blocks cannot be cured.

Controls

Select an infected block to cure it by either clicking or tapping it. Selecting a block that's not infected does nothing.

Score

Cure the blocks in pairs of equal color to increase your score. Successful consecutive cures of color pair results in a bonus.
